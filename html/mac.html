Mac and iPhone

Answer calls or messages from your iPhone directly on your Mac. See and control what’s on your iPhone from your Mac with iPhone Mirroring. Use Universal Clipboard to copy images, video or text from your iPhone, then paste into another app on your nearby Mac. And thanks to iCloud, you can access your files from either your iPhone or your Mac. And so much more.

Mac and iPad

Sketch on your iPad and have it appear instantly on your Mac. Or use your iPad as a second display, so you can work on one screen while you reference the other. You can even start something on your iPad — like a presentation or an email — and continue it on your Mac.

Mac and Apple Watch

Automatically log in to your Mac just by wearing your Apple Watch with Auto Unlock — no password typing required. Your Watch also acts as an instant authenticator, letting you approve app installations, unlock settings, and confirm Apple Pay purchases on your Mac with a quick double-click of the side button. If you ever misplace your computer, you can even use the Find Devices app on your Watch to play a sound and locate your Mac instantly.
